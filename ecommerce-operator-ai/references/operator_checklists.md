# Operator Checklists

## Plain-Language Intake Checklist

Ask the operator to provide:

- Product name and category.
- Real product photos or supplier images.
- Variant list: color, size, bundle, quantity.
- Price, cost, stock, shipping size/weight.
- 3-10 competitor links.
- What customers care about most.
- Any claims that need proof: waterproof, organic, medical, food-safe, child-safe, certified, patented.
- Brand tone: premium, practical, cute, minimalist, professional, etc.
- Target country/language.

## Launch Checklist

- Upload sheet has no empty required fields.
- SKU and variant values are unique and consistent.
- Price is within planned margin and competitor range.
- Main image follows platform rules.
- Bullets do not repeat unsupported claims.
- Search terms avoid irrelevant or unauthorized trademarks.
- Package contents, size, color, and materials match images.
- Compliance notes are resolved or clearly marked before publishing.
- Save final files in a dated folder.

## Daily Competitor Monitoring

Track:

- Price changes and coupons.
- New images/videos.
- Rating and review count movement.
- New negative review themes.
- Stock-out or delivery promise changes.
- New bundles or variants.
